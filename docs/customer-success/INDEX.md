## Certification Tracks

### Tier 1: Certified Associate (Free)
**For:** New users and basic needs
- 50 questions in 45 minutes
- Covers: Setup, basic usage, configuration
- Digital badge included
- Study time: 5-8 hours
- [Full details →](CERTIFICATION-PROGRAM.md#tier-1-certified-associate)

### Tier 2: Certified Professional ($99)
**For:** Advanced users and team leads
- 75 questions + 3 scenarios in 120 minutes
- Covers: Advanced features, optimization, integrations
- Digital badge + certificate
- Study time: 15-20 hours
- Prerequisites: Associate cert + 6 months experience
- [Full details →](CERTIFICATION-PROGRAM.md#tier-2-certified-professional)

### Tier 3: Certified Expert ($299)
**For:** Architects and masters
- 100 questions + 5 scenarios + capstone in 240+ minutes
- Covers: Enterprise architecture, security, advanced optimization
- Elite badge + certificate + directory listing
- Study time: 40-50 hours
- Prerequisites: Professional cert + 2 years experience
- [Full details →](CERTIFICATION-PROGRAM.md#tier-3-certified-expert)
