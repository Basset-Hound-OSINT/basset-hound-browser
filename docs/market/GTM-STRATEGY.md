## Executive Summary

Basset Hound's Wave 15 GTM launches over **4 weeks (Jun 15 - Jul 15, 2026)** with a **phased approach**: soft launch (beta) → early access → product launch → scaling. The goal is to reach **100-150 customers by Dec 31, 2026** and **$3-5M ARR**.

**Launch Timeline:**
- **Week 1 (Jun 1-7):** Planning & preparation
- **Week 2-3 (Jun 8-21):** Beta launch + PR outreach

- **Week 6-7 (Jul 6-20):** General availability + scaling
- **Week 8+ (Jul 21-31):** Growth & expansion

## Part 1: Launch Strategy Overview

### Channel 4: Direct Sales (Enterprise)

**Goal:** Land 5-10 enterprise customers in first 6 months

**Sales Process:**

**Prospecting (Week 1-2):**
- Build target account list: 100 high-value prospects
- Identify decision makers (VP Product, VP Strategy, CISO)
- Research: Understand their competitive challenges
- Initial outreach: Personalized cold email + Slack message (if possible)

**Discovery Call (Week 3-4):**
- Schedule 20-30 discovery calls
- Qualification: Identify budget, timeline, decision process
- Pain points: Understand specific challenges
- Solution fit: Pitch high-level value (15-min call)
- Next step: Request demo/POC meeting

**Demo & POC (Week 5-6):**
- Live demo: Show real monitoring data (anonymized competitors)
- Custom POC: 2-4 week proof-of-concept with their competitors
- ROI presentation: Custom ROI calculator based on their numbers
- Objection handling: Address concerns about evasion, accuracy, integration

**Negotiation & Closing (Week 7-8):**
- Term negotiation: Volume discounts, payment terms, SLA
- Legal review: Contract review (typically 1-2 weeks)
- Closing: Get signature + onboarding

**Expected Results:**
- 20-30 discovery calls/month
- 10-15 demos/month
- 3-5 POCs in progress
- 1-3 deals closed/month
- Target: 5-10 enterprise customers by end of Q3

**Sales Compensation:**
- Base salary: $100K
- Commission: 15% of contract value (annual)
- Example: $50K annual contract = $7,500 commission
- On-target earnings: $150K (salary + commission)

## Part 3: Sales Organization & Process

### Sales Team Structure

**Phase 1 (Now - Jun):** 1 founder-led sales  
**Phase 2 (Jul-Sep):** + 1 enterprise sales rep  
**Phase 3 (Oct-Dec):** + 1 mid-market sales rep  
**Phase 4 (2027):** + 2 more sales reps, sales manager

### Sales Process & Timeline

**Discovery:**
- Method: Cold email, warm intro, inbound
- Duration: 1-2 weeks
- Goal: Qualify opportunity, understand pain, set up demo
- Success metric: 50%+ of outreach converts to discovery

**Demo:**
- Duration: 2-4 weeks
- Format: 30-minute live demo + Q&A
- Content: Show real examples, explain value prop
- Success metric: 50%+ of discovery → demo conversion

**POC:**
- Duration: 2-4 weeks
- Scope: Monitor 5-10 of customer's competitors
- Deliverable: Real monitoring data, report
- Success metric: 60-70% of demos → POC conversion

**Negotiation:**
- Duration: 1-2 weeks
- Focus: Volume discounts, payment terms, SLA
- Owner: Sales + CFO/finance
- Success metric: <10% of POCs fail at negotiation

**Closing:**
- Duration: 1-2 weeks
- Focus: Contract execution, legal review
- Owner: Sales + legal
- Success metric: 100% of negotiated deals → signed

**Total Sales Cycle:** 60-90 days (typical for enterprise)

## Part 6: Risk Mitigation

### Market Risks

| **Risk** | **Probability** | **Impact** | **Mitigation** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Slow customer adoption | 40% | High | Focus on SMB/mid-market first, easier sales cycle |
| Competitors launch similar product | 30% | Medium | Maintain tech lead on evasion, build partnerships |
| Market downturn reduces spending | 20% | High | Build self-serve tier, focus on ROI metrics |
| Key team member leaves | 15% | High | Document processes, cross-train team |

### Execution Risks

| **Risk** | **Probability** | **Impact** | **Mitigation** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sales team doesn't hit pipeline targets | 30% | Medium | Hire experienced sales rep (contract), commission-based |
| Marketing campaigns underperform | 25% | Medium | A/B test messaging, reallocate budget to winners |
| Customer churn higher than expected | 20% | Medium | Improve onboarding, customer success, NPS |
| Product issues delay launch | 15% | High | Maintain feature freeze 2 weeks before launch |
