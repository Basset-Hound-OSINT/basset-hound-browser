## UPGRADE & DOWNGRADE POLICY

### Tier Upgrades
- **Pro-rated upgrade:** If you upgrade mid-year, you only pay for the difference
- **Example:** Upgrade from Starter ($50K) to Professional ($150K) on July 1
  - Remaining Starter cost (6 months): $25K
  - Professional cost (6 months): $75K
  - **Total upgrade fee:** $50K
  - **New annual run rate:** $150K

### Tier Downgrades
- **Pro-rated downgrade:** Applied to next billing period
- **No penalty:** You may downgrade at any time
- **Example:** Downgrade from Professional to Starter at year 2
  - New annual cost: $50K (starting next period)

### Add-On Changes
- **Add anytime:** New add-ons are pro-rated to next billing period
- **Remove anytime:** Remove add-ons with 30-day notice
