## CALCULATION ENGINE: AUTOMATIC ROI COMPUTATION

**Based on your inputs above, here are the calculated returns:**

### COMPONENT 1: REVENUE PROTECTION

**Calculation:**
```
(Competitive changes/week) × 52 weeks × 
(Customers lost/change) × 
(% you'd retain with faster response) × 
(Customer value)
```

**Example with your numbers:**
- Competitors make changes: 6 per week
- Customers lost per change (current): 10
- You'd retain with 10-min response: 8 (80%)
- Customer value: $150
- **Weekly lost revenue if you don't improve:** 6 × 10 × $150 = $9,000
- **Weekly savings from faster response:** 6 × 8 × $150 = $7,200
- **Annual savings:** $7,200 × 52 = **$374,400**

### COMPONENT 2: TEAM EFFICIENCY

**Calculation:**
```
(Hours/week spent) × 
(% that could be automated) × 
(52 weeks/year) × 
(Loaded salary/hour)
```

**Example with your numbers:**
- Hours/week on monitoring: 15
- Could automate: 80% = 12 hours
- Hourly rate (loaded): $75
- **Annual hours freed:** 12 × 52 = 624 hours = 0.3 FTE
- **Annual value:** 624 × $75 = **$46,800**

### COMPONENT 3: COMPLIANCE VALUE

**Calculation:**
```
(Estimated fine if not compliant) × 
(Probability of regulatory finding) +
(Value of avoided penalties and fines)
```

**Example:**
- Estimated fine: $500K
- Probability: 15%
- **Expected value of compliance:** $500K × 15% = **$75,000**

### COMPONENT 4: STRATEGIC VALUE

**Calculation:**
```
(Freed FTE from automation) × 
(Strategic work value impact)
```

**Example:**
- Freed FTE: 0.3
- Can now do strategic analysis worth: $150K per FTE
- **Additional strategic value:** 0.3 × $150K = **$45,000**
