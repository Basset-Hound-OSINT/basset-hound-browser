## OBJECTION HANDLING DEEP DIVES

### Objection 1: "It's too expensive"
**Root Cause:** Usually a budget concern, not a value concern

**Response:**
> "I understand. Let's talk about ROI. You're currently spending [X hours/week] on manual competitive monitoring. That's [Y dollars] in labor costs. Plus you're losing [Z dollars] in revenue due to slow response time. Basset Hound costs [price], but you're saving [value]. That's a [payback months]-month payback. Plus we have flexible payment options and volume discounts. Should we talk about a payment plan that fits your budget?"

**Fallback:** Suggest Starter tier instead of Professional, or offer free trial first

---

### Objection 2: "We have internal tools already"
**Root Cause:** Status quo bias, lack of understanding of limitations

**Response:**
> "That's great that you've invested in internal tools. Most of what makes Basset Hound different is the bot detection evasion - 85-90% success rate getting through detection systems. Plus, we handle updates automatically as detection services change. That usually requires a dedicated engineer to maintain. And our enterprise integrations with Slack, Splunk, etc. are pre-built. What's the biggest limitation you're hitting with your internal tools right now?"

**Fallback:** Offer to supplement their internal tools rather than replace

## COMMON DEMO MISTAKES TO AVOID

❌ **Mistake 1:** Talking too much, not listening
- Fix: Ask questions, listen 60% / talk 40%

❌ **Mistake 2:** Going too deep into technical details too early
- Fix: Save tech details for CTO/VP Eng. Focus on business value with executives.

❌ **Mistake 3:** Skipping the ROI discussion
- Fix: Always show financial impact. Business value is the real reason they'll buy.

❌ **Mistake 4:** Not customizing the demo
- Fix: Use their competitors, their use cases, their language

❌ **Mistake 5:** Leaving without next steps
- Fix: Always end with clear action items and timeline

❌ **Mistake 6:** Overselling or promising what we can't deliver
- Fix: Be honest about limitations. Manage expectations upward.

❌ **Mistake 7:** Not sending demo materials after
- Fix: Email same day, include ROI calc, case studies, pricing

❌ **Mistake 8:** No follow-up
- Fix: Schedule next call before hanging up. Follow up 3x over 2 weeks if needed.
