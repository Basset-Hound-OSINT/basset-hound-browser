================================================================================
CRITICAL QUESTIONS ADDRESSED
================================================================================

Q: Is real-world validation worth delaying the release?
A: Yes. 1 week validation saves 10+ weeks of customer support. Shipping 
   untested creates reputation damage and support burden that costs 100x more.

Q: What if we discover major bugs in real-world testing?
A: That's the point. Better to find them now than with customers. We have 
   3-day buffer in timeline for critical bug fixes.

Q: Will the evasion work against real bot detection?
A: Unknown until tested. v12.6.0 specifically includes Phase 4 (Evasion 
   Testing) to measure real effectiveness. We'll publish honest results.

Q: Can we do this faster?
A: Yes (Option A = 1-2 weeks), but we lose feature value. 2-3 weeks is 
   optimal balance.

Q: What's the minimum viable version?
A: Real-world validation + bug fixes (Phase 1-2 only). But Option C adds 
   features at minimal cost increase.
