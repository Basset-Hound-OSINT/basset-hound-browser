## CRITICAL QUESTIONS ANSWERED

### Q: Is real-world validation worth 1-2 weeks?
**A:** Absolutely. 1 week validation = months of customer support avoided. Shipping untested software is false economy.

### Q: What if real-world testing discovers major bugs?
**A:** That's the whole point. Find them NOW, not with customers. We have 3-day buffer in timeline.

### Q: Can we parallelize more phases?
**A:** Yes - Phase 3 and 4 can run parallel. Phase 1-2 must be sequential (Phase 1 findings inform Phase 2 fixes).

### Q: What if evasion doesn't work against real detection?
**A:** We document it honestly (80% measured, not claimed). We release with transparency about limitations. Improvement is v12.7.0 scope.

### Q: Do we really need 2-3 weeks?
**A:** Yes. Phase 1 (validation) = 3 days non-negotiable. Phase 2 (bugs) = 4 days required. Phase 3-5 = 7-8 days. 2-3 weeks is realistic with team parallelization.

### Q: What's the minimum viable v12.6.0?
**A:** Option A (Quick Validation). But it leaves money on the table. Option C is sweet spot.
